BUY.th

BUY.th is a shopping platform that brings together spiritual items such as amulets, talismans, and sacred objects, alongside exclusive Art Toys from designers and artists, offering a seamless and trusted shopping experience.

NFC Certificate Technology guarantees authenticity—simply scan the NFC tag to instantly verify product details. Connect directly with artists and spiritual craftspeople while shopping with confidence. Where spirituality meets collectible art. Explore our curated collection today.

Role & Responsibilities

UI/UX Design, UI Development

Duration

Apr 2023 – Mar 2024

Tools & Technologies

Figma, Svelte, Expo, Tailwind CSS

Web & App Interfaces

The platform was designed for both web and mobile, ensuring
• Consistent user experiences across devices
• A clean layout to highlight product visuals
• Secure and intuitive user flows for shopping, ordering, and payment

Redesign Initiative

To elevate user experience and strengthen brand identity, we redesigned the mobile application during the migration from a WebView-based framework to Expo. This included updating the logo, refining the color palette, and unifying UI components to ensure a smoother, more visually appealing interface.
